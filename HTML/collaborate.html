Collaborate!

Join us, Be part of the story!

Host your own screening or signature event.  Be a film distributor, helping get the word out, sharing our films, and the innovative way that we work with youth and their communities!

 Volunteer your skills, knowledge, or time in person or remothely.  We can use your film, story, editing skills as well as your language, business, marketing talents to reach our ambitious aim of making youth voice and leadership a worldwide reality!

Fundraise in your own community, with a small house party, or a larger crowd funding campaign.  We have starter booklets to help you plan, or we can work with you to build upon your own creative ideas!

Partner with us to organize your own chapter of USH.

What is in it for you?  Collaborating with us, you gain valuable nonprofit development experience by building social media strategies, planning business models, developing online platforms and initiating marketing and fundraising campaigns.  You also get to be part of a startup organization that is going to change the way indigenous youth learn and lead themselves and their communities!  

We look forward to working with you!!
