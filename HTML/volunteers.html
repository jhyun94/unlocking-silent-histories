OUR VALUABLE VOLUNTEERS

LIKE EXTRAS IN A FILM!

Our volunteers have a passion for working with youth and film. Like extras on a set, these people play key roles to ensure that the productions come to fruition.  They selflessly dedicate their time to various aspects of our organization's development. 

As a Volunteer, you join our creative process in person or remotely. Visit sites, where you apply film, storytelling, editing, entrepreneur, or language skills. Participate from home, helping us reach our ambitious aim of amplifying youth voice and leadership worldwide!

Jerome Atger

Leadership Development
×

A native of Fort Lauderdale, Florida, Jerome comes from a Mexican and French ethnic background. Jerome currently works as a Human Resources Specialist for the Department of the Navy, and has served over 16 years of active and reserve service in the United States Marine Corps. Jerome supported humanitarian assistance and international development efforts with the U.S. Department of Defense from 2007-2011 in Peru and Costa Rica. He is an advocate of international development concepts in Latin America, and he is devoted to serving Indigenous communities. Mary’s University of Minnesota. Currently, Jerome is serving as the Field Director for USH.

Lacey is completing her doctorate degree at the University of Southern California. She teaches Spanish and Portuguese, as well as courses on Latin American culture, history, and politics.
