About us

UnlockingSilentHistories unleashes youth leaders within indigenous communities by opening spaces for critical inquiry and creative expression in the form of documentary shorts.

We foster a learning environment that is constructed with our students and in turn this environment facilitates youth leadership. In the process, we develop critical, creative, analytical, collaborative, and media technology skills.
